Nothing so much shakes the confidence of mankind in the medical profession as unfulfilled
promises; nothing so much strengthens this
confidence, as fair dealing exhibited in an earnest requirement and fearless expression of the

sooner or later, in some measure to influence the unreasonable and ignorant, - much
sooner, indeed, than a warfare carried on in the
arena of empiricism with its own weapons.
