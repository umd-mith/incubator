
REPORT ON HOMEO'PATHY:
MADE TO TIE COUNSELLORS OF THE MASSACHUSETTS MEDICAL
SOCIETY IN FEBRUARY, 1854.
THE committee appointed by the Counsellors
of the Massachusetts Medical Society, to consider the resolution of the Essex North District
Society,* and also that of Dr. Spofford, in relation to the subject of Homceopathy, beg leave
to Report:That the Massachusetts Medical Society was
incorporated mainly for the purpose of establishing a proper standard of medical education,
and of insuring a competent degree of knowledge among those who should be authorized to
practise the profession of medicine in this Commonwealth, and they are not aware that the
Society possess any power to coerce men, after
* These resolutions contemplated dissolving the connection of
Homoeopathists with the Society.
