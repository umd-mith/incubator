AFFECTIONS OF THE EAR AND OF THE
CHEEKS.
THE EAR.
THE ear is an organ, the importance of which is
recognized by all.
It is subject to various affections.
1. Earache.
2. Inflammation of the Ear.
3. Discharge from the Ear.
4. Swelling of the Cheek.
5. Enlargement of the Parotid and Submaxillary
Glands.
One affection very common is
EARACHE, OR OTALGIA.
This affection is sometimes very troublesome and
difficult to cure. Various remedies are applicable.
